What is dry ice cleaning

Dry ice (solid CO₂) is projected in a controlled manner onto the surface. On impact, contamination is removed while the dry ice sublimates directly into gas, leaving no secondary residue.

Why it’s ideal for engines

This method is used when efficient cleaning is required without abrasion, without water, and without affecting sensitive or precision components.

Applications

Engines & engine bays

Oil deposits, heavy grime, hard-to-reach areas.
